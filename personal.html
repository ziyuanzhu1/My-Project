My Favorite Memories

Click on the picture for full size!

One of my favorite memories was going to EDC Las Vegas with my Babson friends. It was my first time traveling across the country for a festival. Although the 3-day festival was tiring, I truly had one of the best times since I was around music I loved!
Last year, my friends and I would often go to Boston for rave concerts. If not every week, we would go every two weeks! One of the most memorable concerts would be bumping into one of my favorite DJs ARMNHMR. We were able to get pictures with them as well!
During my birthday weekend, my friends and I went clubbing since all of us were finally 21. Since we were all legal enough to attend a club, we even booked a table as well! One of my favorite DJs NGHTMRE played the night of at Royale Boston!.
The first EDM festival I attended is Electric Zoo, which is located in New York. It was my first time attending an EDM festival with so many people! Even though many people got lost throughout the day, we all still had a fun night!
